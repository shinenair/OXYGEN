For each month: cylinders purchased and their cost (from Stock Inward invoices, above) compared against actual bank debits tagged with the LPG Inventory expense category on the Transaction Ledger. This is calculated fresh every time you open this tab — nothing here is manually entered or stored separately, so it can never drift out of sync with your actual records. A mismatch usually means an invoice hasn't been recorded yet, or a bank transaction hasn't been tagged with the LPG Inventory category.
A month's reading is only taken in the first week of the following month, and residents pay around that same time — so "Collected from Residents" here looks at payments recorded the month after the one selected (e.g. January's consumption is matched against February's collections). It draws from the same central Payments ledger used everywhere else in OXYGEN (Fees Received, Dashboard), just offset by one month to correctly match income against the consumption it's paying for. Cylinder cost is estimated as (cylinders used that month) × (running average cost per cylinder across all purchases to date) — a simple, transparent costing method, not FIFO/LIFO batch tracking.
